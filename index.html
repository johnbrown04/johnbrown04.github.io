HPV Vaccination to Prevent Cancer: Awareness, Access, and Uptake among University Students

In-Progress

Project Overview: This research project aims to identify percieved beliefs amongst students about the HPV vaccine, and the use to a series of short-form videos to try to drive vaccination by educating on benefits and myths.

Vaccination Health Communication Study Recruitment Percieved Barriers

Decoding Sexual Agency: The Influence of Gender and Sexual Orientation among 18–20-Year-Olds

In Progress

Project Overview: This research explored the impact gender and sexual orientation have on sexual agency, which is defined as "the capacity to initiate, accept, refuse, and negotiate sexual activity in line with one’s preferences and boundaries."

Results found that one's sexual orientation has more of an effect on sexual agency than gender does.

Gender and Sexual Orientation Sexual Agency

Theatre as a Tool for Health Promotion and Disease Prevention

In Progress

Project Overview: For my Theatre Arts double major capstone project, I decided to blend both of my passions together and analyze how theatre can be used as a public health promotion and communication tool. The arts, theatre specifically, are historically undervalued in terms of cultural impact, and using it as a tool to educate about various public health topics is a strong avenue for future health communicators to take advantage of.
